================================================================================

As a security measure, your physical GXS FlexiCard is disabled for overseas usage by default.
To change this setting:
On the GXS Bank app FlexiCard homescreen, tap on 'Manage'.
Toggle 'Overseas Transactions' to enable or disable this feature.
You can follow these steps anytime to enable or disable your GXS FlexiCard for overseas use.
