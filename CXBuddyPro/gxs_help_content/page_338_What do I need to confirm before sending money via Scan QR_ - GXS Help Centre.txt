For individuals:
Check that your recipient's mobile number and registered name displayed on the screen are accurate before proceeding with your transaction. Confirm with the recipient if you're not sure!
For merchants:
You can check the merchant's UEN number and registered name displayed on the screen before proceeding with your transaction. Be sure to ask the merchant if you're having doubts!
