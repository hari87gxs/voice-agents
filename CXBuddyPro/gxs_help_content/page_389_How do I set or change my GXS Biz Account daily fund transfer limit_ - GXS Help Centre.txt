When you sign up with us, your daily transfer limit is automatically set to S$10,000. You can change this limit to up to S$100,000 per day after 12 hours.
To change your transfer limit on the GXS Bank app,
1. Go to 'Profile'.
2. Tap on 'Change limits'.
