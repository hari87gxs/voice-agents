The GXS Business Banking CNY2025 Cashback Campaign is open to all new and existing sole proprietors to GXS Business Banking.
Simply open or hold a GXS Biz Account and deposit fresh funds of minimum S$10,000 by 28 February 2025, and ensure a day end balance of at least S$10,000 on 31 May 2025.
You will earn a S$88 cashback reward, which will be deposited to your GXS Biz Account by 30 June 2025.
OR
You can open or hold a GXS FlexiLoan Biz account and take a loan by 28 February 2025. Here are the different cashback rewards you can earn:
Important thing to note! You will need to maintain more than 50% of your loan amount three months from the date you first took the loan.
The cashback reward will be credited to your GXS Biz Account by 30 June 2025!
Conditions
Do note that each customer will be eligible for one cashback reward only (of the higherÂ value). And the cash reward is limited to the first 500 eligible customers.
Example
On 25 February 2025, you deposit fresh funds of S$15,000 into your GXS Biz Account and maintain the funds until 31 May 2025.
On the same day, you also borrow a loan for S$20,000 and maintain 50% of the loan till 25 May 2025.
You will be eligible to earn 1 reward - S$388 (higher reward value)
