Your end-of-day account balance must remain at or above S$100,000 for 60 consecutive days. If the end-of-day balance drops below this amount at any time during the 60-day period, you will not be eligible for the reward under this promotion.
However, you will still receive the usual daily interest of 1.08% p.a.
