================================================================================

You'll be pleased to know that no documents are required to be uploaded when opening a GXS Biz Account with us. If we require additional documents, we will email or send you a notification on the GXS Bank app.
Your individual details, business or sole proprietorship information will be extracted from Singpass MyInfo and ACRA respectively.
