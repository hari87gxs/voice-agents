Just download the GXS Bank app via the App Store or Google Play Store. Then on the guest lobby, tap 'Sign Up' for GXS FlexiCard.
If you already have an account with us, log into the GXS Bank app, go to the 'Home' tab, and tap 'Explore' for GXS FlexiCard.
