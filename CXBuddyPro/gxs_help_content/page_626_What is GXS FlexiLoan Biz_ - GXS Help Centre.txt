When you apply for GXS FlexiLoan Biz, you get access to a revolving line of credit from which you can borrow loans from.
What is a revolving line of credit?
You have the flexibility to borrow a loan from the credit line only when you need it
No interest or fees will be charged if you do not borrow from the credit line
You can borrow multiple loans up to your credit limit
What are the benefits of GXS FlexiLoan Biz?
Enjoy low interest rates of up to 4.99%p.a.(EIR 9.32%p.a.)
Borrow instantly at the click of a button
Before borrowing a loan, all interest rates and fees are clearly displayed to you so you can make the best choice
Flexibility to select a monthly repayment date that works for your business
No early repayment fees
No annual fees
No late fees, but a late interest is chargeable if you do not make your loan repayments on time
You can borrow up to 10 loans from your available credit limit
* Effective Interest Rate (EIR) for GXS FlexiLoan Biz is calculated based on an average loan amount of S$10,000 with a 24-month repayment period, from 1 January 2024 to 1 January 2026.
