You are eligible for the insurance coverage if you meet the following requirements:
- You are between 19 and 75 years old Age Next Birthday (both ages inclusive);
- You are a unitholder of the Fullerton SGD Cash Fund (Share Class G) purchased through GXS Bank Pte. Ltd.;
- You are a Singaporean citizen, Singapore Permanent Resident or holders of valid Singapore employment pass but excludes work permit holders;
- You are a resident of Singapore;
- You are not a Prohibited Person (as defined in the Insurance Product Summary); and
- Your application for cover under this Group Personal Accident Plan has been approved by Singlife
