To access documents related to Fullerton SGD Cash Fund, simply follow these steps:
If you have an existing GXS Invest account:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app and proceed to your GXS Invest home screen.
2. Tap on ‘More’ and select the respective document.
If you do not have a GXS Invest account:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app.
2. Tap on ‘Explore GXS Invest’.
3. Scroll down and tap on ‘fund documents’.
Alternatively, you can also access the fund documents via Fullerton Fund Management’s official website (www.fullertonfund.com).
