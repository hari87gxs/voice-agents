Applicable to retail accounts only
eGIRO is a joint initiative by The Association of Banks in Singapore (ABS) and participating banks, with the support of the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS). eGIRO offers customers a convenient way to connect their external bank accounts and add funds to their GXS Savings Account effortlessly.
It's all about making banking smoother and more connected for you!
