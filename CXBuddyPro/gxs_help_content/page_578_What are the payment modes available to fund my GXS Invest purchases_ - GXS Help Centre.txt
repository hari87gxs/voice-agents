Currently, the only payment method available is through the Main Account of your GXS Savings Account.
To purchase Fullerton SGD Cash Fund using your GXS Savings Account, follow these steps:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app and go to the GXS Invest home screen.
2. On the ‘Buy Order’ page, select GXS Savings Account as your payment method. Ensure you have sufficient balance in the Main Account of your GXS Savings Account.
3. Review your order details on the order summary page.
4. Acknowledge that you have read the Invest Account Terms, Fund Documents, and Risk Disclosures.
5. Tap ‘Buy’ to complete the purchase.
