Simply download the GXS Bank app through the iOS App Store or Google Play Store and tap 'Sign up' for GXS FlexiLoan.
If you already have a GXS account:
Log into your GXS Bank app.
Go to the 'Home' tab.
Tap 'Explore' for GXS FlexiLoan.
Applying for GXS FlexiLoan enables you to borrow an Instalment Loan or Balance Transfer based on your needs.
