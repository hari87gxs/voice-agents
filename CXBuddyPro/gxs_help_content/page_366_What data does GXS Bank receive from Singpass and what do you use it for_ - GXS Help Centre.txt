With your consent, GXS receives the necessary information from Singpass Myinfo to onboard you as our customer. Here's the list of data required from Singpass Myinfo:
Full Name
Alias Name
Hanyu Pinyin Name
Nationality
Citizenship
Date of Birth
Country of Birth
Sex
Marital Status
NRIC
Passport Number
Passport Expiry Date
FIN
FIN Expiry Date
Occupation
Name of employer
Annual Income Range
For GXS Business Banking account applicants, your ACRA information will also be shared with GXS Bank.
