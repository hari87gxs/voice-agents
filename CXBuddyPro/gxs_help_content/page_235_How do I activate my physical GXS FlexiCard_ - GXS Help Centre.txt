Here's how you can activate your physical GXS FlexiCard upon receiving it:
Log in to the GXS Bank app.
On the GXS FlexiCard homescreen, tap on 'Activate'.
Confirm and tap the 'Activate Card' button.
Key in the 5-digit verification code found on your physical GXS FlexiCard mailer.
Verify yourself using biometric authentication or your passcode.
Enjoy using your GXS FlexiCard!
