It’s easy to open a GXS Savings Account — all it takes is a few minutes to complete the application. Here’s how you can open a GXS Savings Account:
From your Grab app home page, tap on the 'Finance' tab.
Tap on the 'Activate GXS Savings Account' tile.
Follow the onscreen instructions to start your application.
Once we receive your application, you may receive an email from us requesting additional documents as part of the verification process. Please follow the instructions in the email to proceed with your application. Your GXS Savings Account will be opened once the submitted documents have been reviewed and approved.
You may also refer to "What do I need to open a GXS Savings Account" to find out more about the eligibility criteria.
