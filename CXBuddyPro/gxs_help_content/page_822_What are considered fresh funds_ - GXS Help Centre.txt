For the GXS Business Banking CNY2025 Cashback Campaign, fresh funds refer to new funds deposited into your GXS Biz Account between 20 January and 28 February 2025.
The funds should be newly deposited and not transferred from any existing GXS Bank account, including GXS FlexiLoan Biz.
