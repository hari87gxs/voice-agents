================================================================================

You can view details of your GXS FlexiLoan by simply logging into your GXS Bank app.
Your loan details can be found within the GXS FlexiLoan home screen. You’ll be able to view your available credit limit, interest rate, and any borrowed loan(s).
If you have active loan(s), you’ll also be able to view your monthly instalment(s) amount, upcoming due date, number of instalments paid/remaining, and the remaining amount(s) you have left to pay on your loan(s).
