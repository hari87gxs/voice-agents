================================================================================

As a security measure, your physical GXS Debit Card is disabled for overseas use by default. To change this:
On the GXS Bank app home screen, tap on the debit card icon by your GXS Savings Account balance.
Tap on 'Settings'.
Tap on 'Manage Card'.
Toggle on 'Overseas transactions'.
You can follow the above steps to enable or disable your GXS Debit Card for overseas use at any time.
