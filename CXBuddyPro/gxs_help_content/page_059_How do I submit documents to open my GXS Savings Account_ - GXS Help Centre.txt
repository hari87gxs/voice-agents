================================================================================

Submitting documents to open your GXS Savings Account is simple. Launch your GXS Bank app and you'll be brought to the application page. There will be onscreen instructions to guide you through the process. You can submit documents by snapping pictures with your phone camera, or uploading from your phone gallery.
