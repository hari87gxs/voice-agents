Linking or unlinking your GXS Biz Account with PayNow is straightforward with the GXS Bank app! Here's how:
1. Login to your GXS Bank app. Go to 'Profile'.
2. Under 'Profile', click 'Manage PayNow'.
To register for PayNow:
1. You can register for PayNow with your UEN and/or with a suffix.
2. The PayNow name will be populated according to your company name. This is what others will see when they search for you via UEN.
3. Click 'Confirm' and your registration is complete!
To deregister for PayNow:
In your profile, you will see the that are registered with PayNow. You can select which identifiers to deregister as below:
1. Tap the three dots on the right of the registered identifier.
2. Select 'Deregister' from the pop-up menu.
3. Click 'Yes' to confirm your deregistration. Weâ€™ve also added in 'No' if you change your mind :)
