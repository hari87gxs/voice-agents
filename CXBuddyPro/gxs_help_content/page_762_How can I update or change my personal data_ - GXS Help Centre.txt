================================================================================

Updating your personal data is easy! Log into your GXS Bank app, click on your profile button, and select what you'd like to edit. You can edit your preferred name, email address and mobile information. We may request for additional documents for verification. To update or correct other details, please contact us at:
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: GXS Chat
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: Contact Us form
GXS Hotline: 1800 866 6685 or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
GXS Customer Support Email:
help@gxs.com.sg
You will need to provide the following information and documents listed below:
Types of personal data to be corrected and the correction(s) to be made.
Supporting document(s) for the requested correction(s).
