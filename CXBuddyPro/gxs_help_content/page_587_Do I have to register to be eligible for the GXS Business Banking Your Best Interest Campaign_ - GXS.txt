Good news! You do not have to register to participate in the GXS Business Banking Your Best Interest Campaign.
The promotional rate of 4.99%p.a. (9.32% EIR*) is provided to all new and existing holders of GXS FlexiLoan Biz.
This promotional rate is only applied from 1 March to 30 June 2025 and will revert to the prevailing rate if conditions are not met.
You can refer to the full Campaign Terms and Conditions
here
.
* The Effective Interest Rate (EIR) is the rate that reflects the true cost of borrowing and takes into account total charges. It's computed over a reducing balance basis and accrued daily.
