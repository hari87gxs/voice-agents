We have multiple rewards for GXS Business Banking customers who are new and existing Grab Merchants. Whether you're with GrabPay, GrabFood or GrabMart, there are enticing rewards limited to the first 500 sign-ups.
However, the rewards are exclusive for Grab Merchants, subject to GXS Biz Account opening and linking the account as your Grab Merchant settlement account.
For Existing Deliveries (GrabFood & GrabMart) merchants: Waived 12 km Islandwide Delivery subscription fee for 3 months (U.P from $500/mth for Grab Food , from $280/mth for Grab Mart).
OR
For New to Grab Deliveries (GrabFood & GrabMart) merchants: Activation fee rebate (U.P up to $300).
OR
For New and Existing GrabPay-only merchants: MDR rebate - GrabPay in store (P2M) and online (ONA): 0.5% MDR rebate for 1 month (capped at $300).
