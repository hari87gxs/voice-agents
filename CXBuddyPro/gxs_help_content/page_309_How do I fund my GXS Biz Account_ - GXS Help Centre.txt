You may fund your GXS Biz Account via FAST transfer:
On the GXS homescreen, tap 'Add Money'.
Youâ€™ll see your GXS beneficiary name, account number, bank name, bank code, and branch code.
Log in to the bank account you wish to transfer money from, to fund your GXS Biz Account.
Input your GXS Biz Account's details in another bank account's details to transfer money to your GXS Biz Account.
(Note: At the moment, we do not support the receipt of funds to GXS Biz Accounts via Telegraphic Transfer, Cheque and Non-FAST transactions such as GIRO, MEPS & eGIRO.)
