Boost Pocket offers a bonus interest on top of a base interest. Here are the rates:
Base interest:
1.08% p.a.
Bonus interest:
0.3% p.a. for 3 months
0.22% p.a. for 1, 8 or 12 months
This gives your Boost Pocket a total interest rate of:
1.38% p.a. for the 3 months tenure
1.30% p.a. for the 1, 8 or 12 months tenure
Best of all, the Boost Pocket interest rate remains locked in for the entire tenure!
