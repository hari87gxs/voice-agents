The GXS Business Banking CNY2025 Cashback Campaign refers to the promotion that is running from 20 January to 28 February 2025.
GXS Biz Account holders and GXS FlexiLoan Biz holders who fulfill the campaign requirements will have the chance to receive up to S$588 in cashback reward, which will be deposited to their GXS Biz Account.
