You can view your GXS FlexiCard monthly statements on the GXS Bank app.
On the GXS FlexiCard homescreen, tap on 'Manage'.
Tap on 'Monthly Statements'.
Select a month.
Do note that monthly statements are generated 14 calendar days before your card payment due date.
