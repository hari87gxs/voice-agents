The GXS FlexiLoan application process is hassle-free and you don't need to provide us with any documentation! Just ensure your Singpass Myinfo is up to date and we'll take care of the rest.
Applying for GXS FlexiLoan enables you to borrow an Instalment Loan or Balance Transfer based on your needs.
We'll reach out if there's anything else we need from you.
For assistance on your Singpass access, do reach out to
Singpass Helpdesk
directly.
