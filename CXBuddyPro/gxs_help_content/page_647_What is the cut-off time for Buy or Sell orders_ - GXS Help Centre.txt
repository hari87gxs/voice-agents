This is the specific time on a given dealing day that your transaction will be considered for that day's value. Itâ€™s important to know this time so you can plan and ensure your transaction is recorded correctly. The cut-off time to transact on GXS Invest is 3:30pm every business day.
For Buy orders placed before 3:30pm, your allocated units will be reflected in your account on the next business day.
For Buy orders placed after 3:30pm or on a non-business day, your allocated units will be reflected in your account the following business day instead.
For Sell orders placed before 3:30pm, you will receive your proceeds on the next business day.
For Sell orders placed after 3:30pm or on a non-business day, you can generally expect to receive your proceeds the following business day instead.
