If you’re a Grab or Singtel customer, we may share your data with them if
You consented to third party data sharing with Grab and/or Singtel when you signed up with us.
You consented to Grab and/or Singtel’s usage of your personal data from GXS.
You can choose to stop sharing your data with Grab and/or Singtel anytime – you can do so via the app:
Click on 'Me'.
Select 'Manage data sharing' to withdraw your consent.
Or drop us a call or a chat:
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: GXS Chat (recommended)
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: Submit an enquiry
GXS Hotline: 1800 866 6685 or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
