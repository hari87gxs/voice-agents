================================================================================

Here are several ways to transfer funds to and from your GXS Savings Account:
You can send funds via:
FAST
PayNow
Intrabank (applies to GXS Savings Account only)
You can receive funds via:
FAST
Inter-bank GIRO
PayNow (you must register your GXS Savings Account for PayNow to be eligible)
At the moment, GXS Savings Account is not able to send or receive funds via the following methods: Telegraphic Transfer, Cheque and Non-FAST transactions such as MEPS.
