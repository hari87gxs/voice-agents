All GXS Bank customers are welcome to participate in this campaign!
Simply refer your family and friends to apply for a GXS product (GXS Savings Account, GXS FlexiLoan, or GXS FlexiCard) using your unique referral code, which can be found within your GXS Bank app Message Centre or email from GXS Bank.
