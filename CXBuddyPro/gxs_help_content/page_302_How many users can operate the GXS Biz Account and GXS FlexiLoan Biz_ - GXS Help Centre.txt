================================================================================

Only one authorised person (who is both the director and a shareholder of the company) can operate and manage the GXS Biz Account and GXS FlexiLoan Biz.
To protect your GXS Bank account, do not share your device where the GXS Bank app is logged in, avoid logging in on a different phone, and never share your GXS Bank app credentials.
