Complete information on the insurance coverage is only available to GXS Bank customers. Here is how you can find more information:
If you have an existing GXS Invest account:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app and proceed to the GXS Invest home screen.
2. Tap on ‘More’.
3. Select ‘Singlife Insurance Product Summary’.
If you do not have a GXS Invest account:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app.
2. Tap on ‘Explore GXS Invest’
3. Scroll down and tap on ‘fund documents’ and select ‘Singlife Insurance Product Summary'.
