We’re excited to have you on board! You can apply for an account as long as you
Are at least 16 years old,
Live in Singapore,
Have a valid visa e.g. Dependant’s Pass (DP), Employment Pass (EP), Work Permit, and
Have a SingPass Myinfo account.
Not hold an existing GXS Biz Account and/or GXS FlexiLoan Biz
Make sure that your residential address is updated on SingPass before signing up for an account.
If you require assistance on your Singpass access, you may contact
Singpass Helpdesk
directly.
