The GXS FlexiLoan Biz application process is hassle-free and requires no documentation.
Simply ensure that your company details and residential address are updated on ACRA and Singpass respectively, before signing up for an account.
If you require assistance on your Singpass access, you may contact
Singpass Helpdesk
directly.
