Here are the different ways to transfer funds to and from your GXS Biz Account:
You can send funds via:
FAST
PayNow
You can receive funds via:
FAST
PayNow QR
At the moment, GXS Biz Account is unable to send or receive funds via the following methods:
Telegraphic Transfer
Cheque
Non-FAST transactions such as GIRO, MEPS and eGIRO.
