You will be able to do simple banking activities with GXS right within the Dash app, such as:
Link/unlink GXS Savings Account from Dash app.
Update daily transaction limit for linked GXS Savings Account on Dash app.
View account details of linked GXS Savings Account.
Send money directly from your linked GXS Savings Account.
Top up Dash wallet from GXS Savings Account.
Make QR payments directly from your linked GXS Savings Account.
