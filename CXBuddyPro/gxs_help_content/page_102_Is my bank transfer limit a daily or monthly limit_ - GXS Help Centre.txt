Title: Is my bank transfer limit a daily or monthly limit? - GXS Help Centre

Scraped: 2025-11-28T01:12:32.286588

================================================================================

Your bank transfer limit is the daily limit for all transactions. This includes FAST payments and transfers to both GXS and non-GXS bank accounts. You may change the limit within your GXS Bank app.
