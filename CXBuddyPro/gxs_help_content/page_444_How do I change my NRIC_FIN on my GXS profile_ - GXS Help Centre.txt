================================================================================

We rely on Singpass Myinfo for some of your personal details, so this cannot be changed at the moment. If you’ve changed your NRIC/FIN since signing up with us (e.g. due to a change in residency status), please reach out to us for assistance at:
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: GXS Chat (recommended)
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: Submit an enquiry
GXS Hotline:
1800 866 6685
or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
GXS Customer Support Email:
help@gxs.com.sg
You may be asked to provide proof of NRIC/FIN change via email.
