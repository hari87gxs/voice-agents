================================================================================

If you are transferring an amount that is higher than your daily transfer limit, you can change your limit in two ways:
Go to 'Me'.
Press 'Change limits'.
Alternatively,
From the 'Send' page, tap the three dots.
Select 'Change daily transfer limit'.
