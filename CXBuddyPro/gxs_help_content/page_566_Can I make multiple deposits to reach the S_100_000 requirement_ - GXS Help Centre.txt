Is FAQ Answer: True

================================================================================

Yes. For new customers, your 60-day period will begin on the day your total account balance first meets or exceeds S$100,000. For existing customers, your 60-day period will begin on the day you deposit a total of S$100,000 in fresh funds within the campaign period.
