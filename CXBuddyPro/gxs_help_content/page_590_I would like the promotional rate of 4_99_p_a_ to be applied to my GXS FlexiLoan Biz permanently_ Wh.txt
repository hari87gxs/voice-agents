Simply hold an existing or open a new GXS FlexiLoan Biz and borrow a new loan between 1 March to 30 June 2025.
After which make sure you make timely repayments for the next 3 months.
Fulfill these conditions and the rate of 4.99% p.a. (9.32% EIR) will be applied to any new borrowing of loans thereafter.
