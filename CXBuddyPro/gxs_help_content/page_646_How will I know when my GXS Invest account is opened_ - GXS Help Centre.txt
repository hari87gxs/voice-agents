Once your GXS Invest account is opened, you’ll receive a welcome message and an email. You can also turn on your notifications on your device so as to be notified directly.
To turn on push notifications:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app.
2. On the homescreen, tap on ‘Me’.
3. Select notifications and emails.
4. Toggle 'on' to receive notifications from us.
