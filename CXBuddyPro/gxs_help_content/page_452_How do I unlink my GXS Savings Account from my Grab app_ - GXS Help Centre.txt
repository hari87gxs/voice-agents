You can unlink your GXS Savings Account from the Grab app in less than a few minutes:
From the Grab app home screen, tap on the 'Finance' tab.
On the top right, select the 'Settings' icon.
Choose 'All payment methods'.
Click on 'GXS Savings Account'.
Select 'Unlink your GXS Savings Account'.
Select 'Unlink'.
Tap on 'I’m Sure'. If you change your mind, there's the option of 'Not now' as well :)
