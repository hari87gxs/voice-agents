================================================================================

Absolutely! Receiving payments is super easy using your QR code. Here's how you can show it to your customers:
1. Open the GXS Bank app.
2. Tap on 'QR on the top bar.
3. On 'Receive' within the QR page, you can show your QR code to your customers.
4. Once they've scanned your QR code, you'll receive the money in your GXS Biz Account.
