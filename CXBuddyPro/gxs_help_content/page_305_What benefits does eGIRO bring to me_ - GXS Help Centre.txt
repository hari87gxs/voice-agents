Applicable to retail accounts only
eGIRO is all about enhancing your banking experience and giving you more flexibility! By using eGIRO, you can transfer funds seamlessly from your non-GXS (or external) bank account(s) to your GXS Savings Account via the GXS Bank app anytime you need.
