If the Singpass Face Verification is stuck on the loading screen, there are several troubleshooting steps you can take to resolve the issue.
For iOS users, here are some steps to follow:
Tap on ‘Settings’
Select ‘General’
Select ‘Transfer’ or’ Reset Device’
Select ‘Reset’
Reset Network Settings
Tap on ‘Location & Privacy’
If the problem persists after trying the above steps, you can reach out for further assistance through the following channels:
GXS Hotline: (+65) 6866 6685
GXS Customer Support Email: help@gxs.com.sg
For Singpass related matters, do contact
Singpass Helpdesk
directly.
