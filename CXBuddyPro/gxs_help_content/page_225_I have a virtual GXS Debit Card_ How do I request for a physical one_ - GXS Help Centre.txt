You can request for a physical GXS Debit Card on the GXS Bank app. To do so:
On the home screen, tap on the debit card icon beside your GXS Savings Account balance.
Click on 'Order physical card'.Â Choose from one of the 3 GXS Debit Card designs that we're currently offering
If you need to, update your mailing address. Then tap 'Send My Card'.
Your newly minted card will be with you in 5 working days!
Do note that your virtual and physical GXS Debit Card will bear the same card number.
