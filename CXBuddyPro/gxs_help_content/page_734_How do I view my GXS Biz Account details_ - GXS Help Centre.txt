The home page in the GXS Bank app displays your account number.
To view more details,
Tap on 'Profile' in the app.
Tap on 'View Account Details'.
Here's some useful details you might need:
GXS Bank Code: 9751
GXS Branch Code: 2188 (Sole Proprietorship), 2388 (Private Limited)
Do note that the Branch Code are the first 4 digits of your GXS Biz Account number and forms part of your 11-digit account number
