================================================================================

Yes, you can receive funds in your GXS Savings Account via GIRO.
Here's some useful details you might need:
GXS Bank Code: 9751
GXS Branch Code: 888
Do note that the Branch Code 888 are the first 3 digits of your GXS Savings Account number and forms part of your 10-digit account number.
Note: We are currently unable to accept GXS FlexiLoan repayments via GIRO.
