The annual management fee is a fee charged by the fund manager, Fullerton Fund Management, for the ongoing active management of the Fund, of which a portion is paid to GXS Bank as a distributor.
The annual management fee for Fullerton SGD Cash Fund - Share Class G is 0.25% p.a.
