Thank you for your support!
Simply download the GXS Bank app through the iOS App Store or Google Play Store. Launch the app and select 'Business banking'. After which, tap on 'Sign up' and we will guide you the rest of the way.
Note - we currently do not accept applications from US Citizens, US Permanent Residents and Green Card holders. If we require additional documents, we will email you or send you a notification on the GXS Bank app.
