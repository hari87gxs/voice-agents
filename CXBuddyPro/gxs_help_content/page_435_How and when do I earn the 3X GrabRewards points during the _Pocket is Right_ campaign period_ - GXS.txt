To be eligible for 3X GrabRewards points, you need to open your GXS Savings Account between 1 April to 30 Jun 2025 and ensure your GXS Savings Account is linked to the Grab app. Your spend on the Grab app for Grab Transport, Food, Mart and Express using your linked GXS Savings Account as a funding source in the following two months will qualify for 3X GrabRewards points.
