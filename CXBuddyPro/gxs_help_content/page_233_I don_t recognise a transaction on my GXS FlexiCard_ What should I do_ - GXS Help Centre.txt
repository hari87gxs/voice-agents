You can raise a dispute for the transaction via the GXS Bank app. Here's how:
Log in to the GXS Bank app.
On the GXS FlexiCard homescreen, tap on 'Manage'.
Tap on 'Get more help' and select 'Raising a card dispute'.
Fill in the form and provide any details or documents to support the investigation.
If you require further assistance, reach out to us via the GXS Help Centre:
GXS app, Help Centre: GXS Chat (Recommended)
GXS app, Help Centre: Submit an enquiry
GXS Hotline: 1800 866 6685 or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
