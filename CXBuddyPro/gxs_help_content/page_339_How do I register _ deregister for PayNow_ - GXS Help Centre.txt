Applicable to retail accounts only
Linking or unlinking your account can be done easily within the app, simply perform the following:
Login to your GXS Bank app. Go to 'Profile'.
Under 'My payment settings', click 'Manage PayNow'.
To register for PayNow:
You can choose to register with your phone number or NRIC.
Enter a PayNow nickname. This is what others will see when they search for you via NRIC/phone number.
Click 'Confirm' and your registration is complete!
To deregister for PayNow:
In your profile, you will see the identifiers (NRIC or phone number) that are registered with PayNow. You can select which identifiers to deregister as below:
Tap the three dots on the right of the registered identifier.
Select 'Deregister' from the pop-up menu.
Click 'Yes' to confirm your deregistration. We’ve also added in 'No' if you change your mind :)
