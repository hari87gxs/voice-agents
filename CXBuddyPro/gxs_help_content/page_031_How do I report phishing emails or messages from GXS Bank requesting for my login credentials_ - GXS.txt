================================================================================

If you did not share your credentials,
Forward the email and screenshots of the messages (containing the contact details) to
report.phishing@gxs.com.sg
. We’ll get in touch with you if we need more information. You can also refer to the official Singapore Police Force website for the latest advisory.
If you've shared your credentials,
Reach out to us immediately so we can suspend your account and take you through the fraud reporting process:
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: GXS Chat (recommended)
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: Submit an enquiry
GXS Hotline: 1800 866 6685 or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
2. Lodge an online police report at the Singapore Police Force website or visit your nearest police Centre.
You can also refer to the official Singapore Police Force website for the latest security advisory.
