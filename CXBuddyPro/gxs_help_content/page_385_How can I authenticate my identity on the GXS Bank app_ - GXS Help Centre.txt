To verify your identity on your current device,
You can key in your 6-digit passcode or
Use Face ID or Touch ID if you've enabled biometric access.
If you're logging in to the GXS Bank app on a new device,
You'll need to verify your identity via Singpass,
Key in the OTP sent to your registered email, and
Key in your 6-digit passcode.
If you are encountering issues with your Singpass login, do have a read
here
