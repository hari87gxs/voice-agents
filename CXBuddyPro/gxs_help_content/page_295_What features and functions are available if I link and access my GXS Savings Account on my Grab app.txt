You'll be able to do simple activities with GXS Bank right on the Grab app, such as:
Link/unlink GXS Savings Account on your Grab account.
Update daily transaction limit for linked GXS Savings Account on Grab account.
View GXS Savings Account details and transaction history for Grab-related activities only.
Top up your GrabPay wallet with GXS Bank.
Make payments directly from your GXS Savings Account and earn GrabCoins for the following Grab activities:
- Grab Transport
- Grab Food orders
- Grab Mart orders
- Grab Express orders
