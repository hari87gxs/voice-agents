You can pay your GXS FlexiCard bill via the following method(s):
Using your GXS Savings Account to pay via the GXS Bank app:
On the GXS FlexiCard homescreen, click on 'Pay Due'
Select the amount you'd like to repay
Choose 'GXS Savings Account' as your payment method
Review your payment details and click 'Confirm & Pay'
Using an external bank account to pay with FAST transfer:
Log in to the GXS Bank app to check your GXS FlexiCard bill outstanding amount and retrieve your card/account number
Proceed to your external bank app/online banking platform to perform a FAST transfer to your GXS FlexiCardÂ number
Ensure you choose FAST payment to a bank account, not credit card account.
Enter your 16-digit GXS FlexiCard number in the bank account field
Note: FAST transfers are almost instantaneous!
