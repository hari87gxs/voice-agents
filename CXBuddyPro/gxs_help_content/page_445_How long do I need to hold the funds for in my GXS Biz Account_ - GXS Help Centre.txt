To qualify for the reward under this campaign, you will need to hold the funds for at least 60 days in your GXS Biz Account from and including the date of depositing funds. Funds must be deposited between 11 November and 31 December 2025.
For example, if you deposit your funds on 15 December 2025, you will need to hold the funds until 13 February 2026 in order to qualify for the reward.
