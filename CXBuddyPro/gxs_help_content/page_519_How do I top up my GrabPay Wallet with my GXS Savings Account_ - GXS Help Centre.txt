We're working on making top-ups to your GrabPay Wallet easy peasy. Here's how you can top up your GrabPay Wallet with your GXS Savings Account:
First, get the VPA link from Grab app. Here's how:
From your Grab app home screen, tap on your GrabPay Wallet balance.
Tap on 'Top Up'.
From the Top Up menu, tap on 'Banking app' > PayNow VPA (If you are doing this for the first time, you should receive a prompt about linking your VPA. Tap on Next > Link VPA).
Select 'PayNow VPA'.
Select 'Other Banks'.
Copy VPA.
Next, send money from your GXS Bank app using the VPA link:
On your GXS Bank app home screen, tap on 'Send Money'.
Select 'VPA'.
Paste the copied VPA in the top bar.
Enter your top-up amount and send.
Top up to your GrabPay Wallet is done!
