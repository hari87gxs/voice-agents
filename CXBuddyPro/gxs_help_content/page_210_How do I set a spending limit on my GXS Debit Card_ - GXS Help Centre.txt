================================================================================

On the home screen of your GXS Bank app, click on the debit card icon by your GXS Savings Account balance.
Tap on 'Settings'.
Select 'Manage Card'.
Tap 'Edit daily transaction limit'.
Adjust your limit and click on 'Confirm'.
From here on, card transactions that will lead to you exceeding your daily limit won't be approved.
