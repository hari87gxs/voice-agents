Title: Can I overpay my monthly repayment amount? - GXS Help Centre
URL: https://help.gxs.com.sg/GXS_FlexiLoan/GXS_FlexiLoan_Repayment/Can_I_overpay_my_monthly_repayment_amount%3F
Scraped: 2025-11-28T01:15:19.283811

================================================================================

Yes, you may repay more than the monthly repayment amount stipulated without incurring fees. The excess amount will be deducted from your next monthly repayment amount due, or applied to your other active loan(s).
