Yes, you can definitely schedule one-time or recurring FAST/PayNow transfers for a future date. Hereâ€™s how to do it in the GXS Bank app:
1. Open your GXS Bank app.
2. Tap on "Send Money" on the top bar.
3. Select method of transfer.
4. Enter the recipient's Account number or proxy details.
5. Under "Types of transfers", choose "Scheduled transfer" and enter a name for the scheduled transfer.
6. Select the transfer frequency.
7. Set the start date.
8. Select number of times to transfer.
9. Review and approve the transfer schedule summary.
