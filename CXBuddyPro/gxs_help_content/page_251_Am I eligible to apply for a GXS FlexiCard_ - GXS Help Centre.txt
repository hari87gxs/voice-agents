You will be eligible to apply for a GXS FlexiCard when you meet all of the following criteria:
Age: between 21 - 55 years
Nationality: Singapore Citizen/ Singapore Permanent Resident
Do not have a GXS FlexiLoan account or Grab DAX PCA (Partner Cash Advance) account or GXS Biz Account and/or GXS FlexiLoan Biz
While there is no income requirement to apply for a GXS FlexiCard, do note that all GXS FlexiCard applications go through an evaluation process which is aligned with our internal requirements.
