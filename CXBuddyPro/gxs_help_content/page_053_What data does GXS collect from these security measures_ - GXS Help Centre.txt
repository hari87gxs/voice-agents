We take your privacy seriously.
The enhanced security feature does not collect any additional personal data. Our enhanced security feature detects potential malware that might put your personal and banking information at risk and informs only you of the apps that could compromise your data.
We do not collect or store any information about the apps installed on your mobile device and how you are using them.
