We use your data to:
Confirm your identity.
Respond to queries and feedback.
Enhance our products and services.
Execute our day-to-day business and administrative operations.
Comply with laws and legal obligations, and our GXS Bank policies.
Perform checks so as to prevent and detect crime, and for risk management purposes.
Note that this is a non-exhaustive list. For more details, please refer to our full Data Protection Policy on GXS Bank website.
