================================================================================

Please inform GXS Bank immediately if there is any change in your company's shareholder or shareholding. You can do this by submitting a copy of your latest ACRA Business Profile through the GXS Bank app. Go to the Help Centre and select 'Submit an enquiry.' After reviewing your documents, we will contact you with the next steps.
