Follow the steps below to find details of your fund holdings:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app.
2. Go to the GXS Invest home page.
Here you can view the details of your investment including the current market value, total returns as well as your latest unit holdings and unit price.
3. Tap on the ‘i’ button next to each field for more information.
