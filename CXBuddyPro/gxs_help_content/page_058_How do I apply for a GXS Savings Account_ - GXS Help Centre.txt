*Thank you for your support!
Simply download the GXS Bank app from Apple Store or Google Play store. Launch the app, and tap on GXS Savings Account ‘Sign up now’. We will guide you the rest of the way.
You'll need to:
Be at least 16 years old,
Have a residential address in Singapore,
Be a Singapore citizen, Singapore Permanent Resident or have a valid visa e.g. Dependant’s Pass (DP), Employment Pass (EP), Work Permit, and
Have a Singpass Myinfo account.
Not hold an existing GXS Biz Account and/or GXS FlexiLoan Biz
Note - we currently do not accept applications from US citizens.If we require additional documents, we will email you or send you a notification on the GXS Bank app.
Your GXS Savings Account will be opened shortly after your documents have been reviewed and approved. We would like to share that your deposits with GXS bank are aggregated and insured up to S$100,000 by the Singapore Deposit Insurance Corporation (SDIC).
If you require assistance on your Singpass access, you may contact
Singpass Helpdesk
directly.
