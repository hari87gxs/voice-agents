Daily transfer limit on GXS Bank app:
This refers to daily amount you can transfer from your GXS Savings Account to other bank accounts, through the various modes of transfer (e.g. PayNow, local bank transfers).
Daily transaction limit for linked GXS Savings Account on Dash app:
This refers to the total daily transactions you can make from your GXS Savings Account on Dash app, including top-ups to your Dash wallet.
Please note that the transfer and transaction limits are not related.
