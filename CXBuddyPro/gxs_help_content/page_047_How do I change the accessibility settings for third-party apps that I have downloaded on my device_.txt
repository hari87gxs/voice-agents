Accessibility settings is currently available on Android devices. The steps to change accessibility settings for third-party apps differ for different phone models.
Generally, the steps are as follows:
Tap on your device’s setting app
Scroll down and select ‘accessibility’
Scroll until you find the accessibility service or feature you need to disable
Tap the toggle switch to turn off the feature
Via Home Screen shortcut
Press and hold on an empty spot on your home screen
Tap on the ‘widgets’ icon
Swipe through the widgets until you reach the ‘settings shortcut’ widget
Drag and drop the widget onto your home screen
Choose 'accessibility’ from the setting options
Tap on the feature you need to disable. Tap the toggle switch to turn off the feature
Note: The steps outlined above are the most common steps. Do refer to your user guide if you encounter any difficulties or variations depending on your specific device model.
