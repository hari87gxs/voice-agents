This security measure is implemented for all GXS Bank app users such as you to ensure the security of your account and to prevent unauthorised transactions.
You cannot opt out of or shorten the cooling-off period.
Furthermore, our Customer Support Team cannot override or bypass this cooling-off period under any circumstances. We appreciate your understanding and patience in helping us maintain the security of your account.
