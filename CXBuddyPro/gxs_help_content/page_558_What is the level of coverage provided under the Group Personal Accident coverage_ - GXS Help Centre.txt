================================================================================

You will be covered for a sum assured of up to three times the value of your investment value in the Fullerton SGD Cash Fund (Share Class G) on the date of accident, subject to a maximum of S$100,000.
Should you be 64 years old and above at the time of accident, your sum assured shall be reduced to 50% of benefits payable, subject to a maximum of S$50,000.
