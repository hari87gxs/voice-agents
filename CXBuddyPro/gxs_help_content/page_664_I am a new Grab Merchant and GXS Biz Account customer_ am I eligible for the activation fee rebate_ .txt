================================================================================

New Grab Merchants who have onboarded onto GrabFood/Mart using a GXS Biz Account as their settlement account during the onboarding process will qualify for the activation fee rebate, which is limited to the first 500 sign-ups by GXS Biz Account customers.
Successful merchants will be notified by Grab via email.
