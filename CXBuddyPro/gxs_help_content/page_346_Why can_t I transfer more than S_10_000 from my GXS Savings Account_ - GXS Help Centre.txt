With the increasing number of scams in Singapore, we’ve put in a temporary measure to safeguard your funds.
What does this mean for you:
For the first 14 days after you've opened a GXS Savings Account, there is a daily transfer limit of S$10,000 within a 24-hour period.
All other account functions remain unchanged.
