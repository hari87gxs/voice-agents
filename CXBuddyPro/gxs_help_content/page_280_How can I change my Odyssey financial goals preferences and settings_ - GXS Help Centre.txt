It's simple! Change your goal preferences via the Settings page. Here's how:
Go to the Odyssey homepage.
Click on the ellipsis (...) on the top right hand corner.
Click on 'Edit Preferences'.
Click on 'Financial Goals'.
Make your changes and click 'Save'.
