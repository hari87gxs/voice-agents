Applicable to retail accounts only
You can do that easily by following these steps:
On the home screen, tap on ‘Send Money’.
Under ‘Recent’ tab, scroll to find the recipient record that you'd like to add as a favourite.
Tap on the star icon next to this recipient.
Provide a ‘Preferred Name’ between 2 and 30 characters, then save the recipient as 'Favourite'.
