================================================================================

If you are an existing GXS user, but are a new GXS Savings Account, GXS FlexiLoan or GXS FlexiCard holder, you will still be eligible for the cashback reward for that new product!
For example, if you already hold a GXS Savings Account but do not have a GXS FlexiCard, you will be eligible for the GXS FlexiCard "Spend Cashback" reward.
