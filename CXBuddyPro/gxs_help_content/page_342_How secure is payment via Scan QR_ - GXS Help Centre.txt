Applicable to retail accounts only
Your payments via GXS Bank app are secure:
All data is encrypted.
You'll have to log in to the GXS Bank app via passcode or biometric authentication before you can transfer funds with Scan QR.
Since Scan QR uses PayNow, all security requirements align with PayNow guidelines.
