Just for signing up a new product with GXS Bank (GXS Savings Account, GXS FlexiLoan or GXS FlexiCard), you will get a S$2 off Singtel Worldwide ReadyRoam/Other voucher. How's that for a little extra love!
You will receive your promo code within 2 calendar days of successful sign up.
Here’s how you can redeem your promo code:
Step 1: Open My Singtel app and select Roaming.
Step 2: Choose your travel destination and ‘Next’.
Step 3: Select ReadyRoam Worldwide or Others.
Step 4: Select your travel date and ‘Review Order’.
Step 5: Key in your promo code and click ‘Apply’. Review and complete your subscription.
Do note the redemption period is valid till 31 March 2025.
Limited to the first 10,000 redemptions, so fastest fingers first!
