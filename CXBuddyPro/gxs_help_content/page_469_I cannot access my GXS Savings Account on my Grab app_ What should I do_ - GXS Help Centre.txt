Fret not! You may contact the Grab Help Centre for assistance if you have trouble accessing your GXS Savings Account on the Grab app.
Nonetheless, you can continue to access your GXS Savings Account on the GXS Bank app. If you're facing an issue accessing your account on the GXS Bank app, do reach out to us and we'll be glad to help:
GXS Hotline: 1800 866 6685 or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
