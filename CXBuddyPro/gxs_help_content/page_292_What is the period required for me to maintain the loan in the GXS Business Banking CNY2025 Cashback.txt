In order to be eligible for the S$188 or S$388 or S$588 cashback reward, you must borrow the required minimum loan amount from your GXS FlexiLoan Biz by 28 February 2025 and maintain more than 50% of your loan amount three months from the date you first took the loan.
Do note that the cashback reward is limited to the first 500 eligible customers. The first 500 customers are selected on a first-come, first-served basis among those who meet the criteria by either depositing fresh funds into their GXS Biz Account or borrowing a loan from their GXS FlexiLoan Biz account.
