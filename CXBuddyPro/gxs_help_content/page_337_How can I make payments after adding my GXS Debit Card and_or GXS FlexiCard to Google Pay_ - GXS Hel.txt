You can make payments on your GXS Debit Card and/or GXS FlexiCard through Google Pay in the following 2 ways:
Pay with your phone in stores or at merchant POS terminals:
Unlock your phone where you've added your card on Google Pay.
Place your phone over the contactless reader.
You will see a tick mark on the screen accompanied by a soft tone and a subtle vibration to indicate a successful payment. You'll also be sent a notification for a successful card payment.
Please note this method is only applicable to NFC (Near Field Communication) capable devices. Please ensure your device has NFC capabilities before trying to make contactless payments using Google Pay.
Online checkout:
For any online merchant offering the Google Wallet or Google Pay as a payment method, you can simply choose this payment method to pay using your GXS Debit Card or GXS FlexiCard.
