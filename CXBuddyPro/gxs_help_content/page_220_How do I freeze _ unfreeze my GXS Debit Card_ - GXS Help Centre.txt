You can temporarily stop your active GXS Debit Card from being used by freezing it. If you would like to use your debit card again, simply unfreeze it.
Here's how you can freeze / unfreeze your debit card:
On the GXS Bank app home screen, tap on the debit card icon beside your GXS Savings Account balance.
Click on 'Freeze Card' or 'Unfreeze Card'.
Confirm the Freeze or Unfreeze request.
