================================================================================

Yes, you can. Here’s how:
On the GXS FlexiCard homescreen, select ‘Pay Due’
Key in the amount you’d like to pay
Select your payment method
Review your payment details and click 'Confirm & Pay'
Please note that a flexi fee or a late fee will be incurred if you choose to make a partial payment instead of in full.
