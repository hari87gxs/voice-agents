================================================================================

Absolutely! You can easily remove a scheduled transfer in the GXS Bank app. Here's how:
1. Tap on "Send Money" on the top bar.
2. In the Scheduled Transfers section, tap the ellipsis button next to the scheduled transfer you want to delete.
3. Choose "Remove scheduled transfer."
4. Confirm by tapping "Remove."
Your scheduled transfer will then be removed from the list.
