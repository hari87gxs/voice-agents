You’ll need to submit supporting documents in this process. It’s potentially complex, so please reach out to us for guidance:
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: GXS Chat (recommended)
GXS Bank app, Help Centre: Submit an enquiry
GXS Hotline: 1800 866 6685 or (+65) 6866 6685 (from overseas)
GXS Customer Support Email:
help@gxs.com.sg
In general, you will be asked to provide supporting documents to us via email or mail.
Do note, all documents that are copies of the original documents will need to be certified true copies by any of the following:
Notary Public
Practising Advocate & Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Singapore
Documents Required as Proxy Decision Makers via Lasting Power of Attorney
If you are the Donee appointed via a Lasting Power of Attorney to act on behalf of Donor who lacks mental capacity, please submit the following documents to us:
Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) document
Medical Report for the activation of LPA (dated within the last 6 months)
Original identity document(s) of Donee and Donor
Latest bank statement (dated within the last 3 months) of Deputyship/other bank account in the name of Donor
Documents Required as Proxy Decision Makers via Court Order
If you are the court-appointed Deputy to act on behalf of the individual who lacks mental capacity, please submit the following documents for the account closure.
Court Order appointing Deputy, made pursuant to the Mental Capacity Act
Original identity document(s) of Deputy and individual lacking mental capacity
Latest bank statement (dated within the last 3 months) of Deputyship/other bank account in the name of individual who lacks mental capacity.
Submission of Documents
You may submit a digital copy of the required documents to us at
GXS Customer Support Email: help@gxs.com.sg
The physical copies of the documents will need to be sent to the bank at:
Attention: GXS Operations

After Submission of Documents
Funds can only be released upon our receipt of the required digital and physical documents.
After processing the documents, we will proceed to close the account and transfer any remaining balance to:
the Deputyship/Lasting Power of Attorney account in the name of individual who loses mental capacity or in the absence of this,
other bank account in the name of the individual who lacks mental capacity.
