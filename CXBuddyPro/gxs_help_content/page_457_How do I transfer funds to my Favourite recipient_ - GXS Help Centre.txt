To transfer funds to your Favourite recipient:
1. Open your GXS Bank app.
2. Tap on the 'Send Money' button.
3. You will see a list of Favourites previously saved.
4. Select the recipient you want to transfer to and your recipient's details will be populated for you.
5. Enter the transfer amount and complete your transfer.
