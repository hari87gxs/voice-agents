To be eligible for the sure-win cashback ensure you deposit a minimum of S$2,000 into your Saving Pocket which is named after the featured destination. The sure-win cash reward amount is randomly generated by our system and ranges between S$1.00 to S$20.00.
Do note the sure-win randomised cash reward is limited to one reward per customer, per month. It will be credited to your GXS Savings Account at the end of the holding period.
