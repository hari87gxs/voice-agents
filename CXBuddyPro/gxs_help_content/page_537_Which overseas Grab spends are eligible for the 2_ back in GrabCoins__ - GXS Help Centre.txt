================================================================================

Eligible Grab spends are Grab overseas services for Grab rides, GrabFood, GrabMart orders and GrabExpress Instant Delivery bookings paid using the GXS Debit Card in the the Grab app.
Top-ups to GrabPay wallets are not considered eligible spends.
