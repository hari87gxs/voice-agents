================================================================================

Eligible merchant outlets will only qualify for one reward, which will be communicated by Grab.
Dual service merchants onboard GrabFood/Mart and GrabPay, will receive delivery-related rewards (i.e. waived participation in Islandwide Delivery - up to 12km for 3 months).
