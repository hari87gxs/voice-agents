You can place a Buy or Sell order for Fullerton SGD Cash Fund by following these steps:
1. Log in to the GXS Bank app.
2. Proceed to the GXS Invest home screen.
3. Tap on the ‘Buy’ or ‘Sell’ buttons.
4. Input or select the amount you wish to buy or sell.
5. Select your GXS Savings Account to fund the purchase or to credit the proceeds.
6. Review your order details in the order summary page.
7. Acknowledge that you have read the Invest Account Terms, Fund Documents as well as Risk Disclosures.
8. Then tap on ‘Buy’ to proceed with the purchase or ‘Sell’ to proceed with the redemption.
