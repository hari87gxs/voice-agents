Here's how you can activate your brand new GXS Debit Card!
From the home screen, tap on 'Debit Card' icon.
Tap 'Activate Now' on the blue banner.
Confirm and tap the 'Activate Card' button.
Verify yourself via biometric or passcode.
Key in the 5-digit verification code found on your Debit Card mailer.
Tap 'Activate' again and you are ready to go!
